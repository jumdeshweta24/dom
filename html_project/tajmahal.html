Favourite Places

Taj Mahal

If there was just one symbol to represent all of India. It would be the Taj Mahal

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a revered Sikh gurdwara located in Amritsar, India. It is a symbol of spiritual and cultural significance, attracting millions of visitors annually.

Mysore

Mysore, in Karnataka, India, is celebrated for its historic Mysore Palace and vibrant Dasara festival, showcasing rich cultural heritage and royal architecture. The city is also known for Chamundi Hills and silk sarees.

Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the world's oldest continually inhabited cities, is a spiritual hub on the banks of the Ganges River, known for its ghats and religious ceremonies. It is revered by Hindus and attracts pilgrims and visitors seeking cultural and religious experiences.
